## Background

CMSMart is an online distribution platform designed to help grocery store owners access a wide range of household products more easily. As the brand aimed to reach and attract new users, the team needed a website that could clearly introduce the service, communicate its core value, and build trust from the first visit.

This project focused on designing a modern and informative website that presents CMSMart as a reliable digital solution for grocery owners.

## Problem Statement

New users needed a clear and trustworthy entry point to understand what CMSMart offers, how the service works, and why it is relevant for their business. Without a structured website, the brand had limited space to explain its value proposition, showcase its ecosystem, and guide users toward key actions such as learning more, contacting the team, or exploring the service.

The challenge was to design a website that could balance brand storytelling, service explanation, and user action in a simple and accessible way.

## Goal & Scope

To design a clean, modern, and informative website that introduces CMSMart to new users, communicates its core value, and encourages users to explore the service or contact the team.

## Process & Solution

### Step 1 / Research & Content Understanding
I started by gathering the information provided by the team and researching several websites to understand common patterns for service introduction, product explanation.

This helped define what kind of content CMSMart needed to communicate clearly on the website.

### Step 2  / Define Website Structure & User Journey
After understanding the product and target audience, I mapped the website structure to guide users from brand introduction to deeper product understanding. The flow was designed to help users quickly understand what CMSMart is, why it is useful, and how they can take action.

### Architecture Information
- Home: Introduce value proposition and key benefits
- About Us: Explain company background, vision, mission, journey, and team
- Ecosystem: Show CMSMart’s partners, collaborators, and business network
- Article: Share insights, updates, and useful content to educate users about the industry, product benefits, and business growth opportunities
- Career: Introduce available job opportunities, company culture, and hiring information for people who want to join the team
- Contact Us: provide clear access for inquiries and communication


### Step 3  / Design the Homepage as the Main Entry Point
The homepage was designed as the main entry point to quickly communicate CMSMart’s value. I focused on a clear hero section, benefit highlights, service area, partner logos.

## Summary

The final website created a clearer digital presence for CMSMart and made the brand easier to understand for new users. By organizing the content into a structured website journey, users could quickly learn what CMSMart offers, explore its ecosystem, and access contact information more easily.

The project also helped the team present CMSMart in a more professional and trustworthy way, supporting the brand’s goal of making the confidence process more accessible to users.
